Conference Venue and Transportation

Forum 2026 will be held at Mong Man Wai Building (MMW) LT1&2, the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), Hong Kong, China.

In-campus shuttle bus service for speakers will be provided.

Getting to CUHK

By MTR (Mass Transit Railway): Take the East Rail Line to University Station. CUHK campus is directly accessible from the station (Exit A).

By Taxi: Taxis are widely available throughout Hong Kong. Ask the driver for “The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Sha Tin.”
